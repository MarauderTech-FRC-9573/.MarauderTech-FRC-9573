MaraduerTech 9573 is the FRC team from Mira Mesa High School, San Diego CA!

What is FRC?

First Robotics Competition is a high-paced robotics competition where students have to design, program and build an industrial sized robot to participate in a themed field game in six weeks under strict rules, time and resources. People from all backgrounds, experiences and interests, both technical and non-technical, and invited and encouraged to join.

Why join?

  • Master STEAM (science, technology, engineering, art, math) skills
  • Improve your teamwork, interpersonal and communication skills
  • Learn and use sophisticated software, hardware and tools
  • Understand Gracious Professionalism and Cooperition
